# Privacy Policy for Cove

**Last updated:** 2026-06-12

Cove is a calm, sensory-friendly app made for autistic children. We built Cove to be a private, offline-first space — your child's experience is not tracked, sold, or shared.

## Who this policy is for

This policy is written for the parents and caregivers who set Cove up. Your child uses Cove; you make the decisions about it.

## What we don't collect

We do not collect or store any of the following from you or your child:

- Names, ages, or any personal details you enter during setup.
- Email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information.
- Photos, videos, voice recordings, or other media.
- Location, contacts, or device identifiers used for cross-app tracking.
- Behavioural data, analytics, or usage telemetry from your child's side of the app.

The child profile information you enter during setup — name, age band, sensory preferences, daily routines, feelings check-ins, favourites — is stored **only in the app's memory on your device**. It is not transmitted anywhere. When the app is closed or uninstalled, that information is gone.

## Children's privacy

Cove is designed with the spirit of COPPA (US), the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code, and similar children's-privacy frameworks in mind. The child-facing side of Cove contains:

- No advertising.
- No third-party SDKs that track users.
- No network requests.
- No way for your child to create an account, sign in, or share anything publicly.

If we ever change this, we will update this policy and tell you in the app before the change takes effect.

## Advertising — Parent Zone only

Cove shows advertisements only on Parent Zone screens — the dashboard, settings, insights, guides library, and guide articles. Parent Zone is reachable only after a tap-and-hold gate, which is intended for an adult to operate.

To serve these ads, we use **Google AdMob**. AdMob is a Google service that may receive a limited set of information from the device when an ad is loaded or shown, including:

- A device-resettable advertising identifier (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS).
- Approximate device information (model, operating-system version, language).
- Approximate location derived from your IP address.

Cove requests **non-personalised ads only**, which limits the data AdMob uses for targeting. You can reset or limit the advertising identifier at any time in your device's settings. For full details of how Google handles ad data, see Google's Privacy & Terms at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

No advertising data is associated with your child's profile, because no profile leaves the device.

## Third-party services

The only third-party service Cove uses is **Google AdMob**, and only in Parent Zone. All other features — audio playback, text-to-speech, fonts, animations, illustrations — run on-device and do not call external servers.

## Permissions

Cove does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, calendar, or notifications. The device's built-in text-to-speech engine is used for the AAC board's "Speak" button; the spoken text is processed on-device by your operating system and is not sent to Cove or to any third party.

## Data storage

All app state — child profile, routines, sensory preferences, favourites, feelings tracking — is held in memory only and is lost when the app is closed. Cove does not currently persist any of your data to disk or to the cloud. Audio and image assets are bundled with the app and do not need to be downloaded.

If a future version of Cove adds on-device persistence (so the app remembers your settings between launches), this policy will be updated before the change ships, and the persisted data will still stay on your device.

## Your choices

- **Limit ad tracking:** open your device settings, find Ads / Privacy, and toggle "Opt out of personalised ads" or "Limit ad tracking." Cove already requests non-personalised ads, but this gives you extra control.
- **Reset everything in Cove:** Parent Zone → "Re-run setup" clears the profile, routines, preferences, and any tracked feelings or visits, and starts onboarding over.
- **Uninstall:** removing Cove from your device wipes everything Cove has stored, because Cove keeps nothing in the cloud.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Cove handles data, we will update this policy and bump the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be flagged in the app the next time you open Parent Zone.
